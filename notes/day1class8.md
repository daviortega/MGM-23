# IMG Submission & Annotation Pipeline [Live Demo]
*by Marcel Huntemann*



Submission seems to be *simple*

## Annotation pipeline:

### Predictions tools:

- CRISPR - ClostridiumtRNAs
- tRNAs - Cmsearch (slow)
- rRNAs - Hmmearch
- Infernal (Blastn, Cmsearch)
- CDS prediction - GeneMark and Prodigal

### Annotation:

 - COG and KOG
 - KEGG and MetaCyc
 - Pfam-A and TIGRfam
 - InterPro Scan
 - Product name assignment
